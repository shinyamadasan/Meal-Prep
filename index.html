How to use Meal Prep Planner

Your weekly meal-prep loop, in 6 steps:

  1. 1. Add your recipesMy Recipes → Add New Recipe (or Paste / Import CSV). Each recipe holds ingredients, nutrition, cost, and fridge/freezer life.
  2. 2. Plan your weekWeekly Planner → tap a meal slot on any day → pick a recipe. Use the “Add to” day chips to fill several days at once.
  3. 3. Get your grocery listIt’s built automatically from your plan, grouped by category with totals. Check items off as you shop.
  4. 4. Cook & mark “✓ Cooked”After cooking, tap ✓ Cooked on the recipe or planner. It deducts the ingredients from your pantry and saves the batch to your fridge.
  5. 5. Track freshnessMy Fridge shows everything you’ve stored and how many days before it goes bad — with warnings when something’s about to expire.
  6. 6. Pantry & pricesTrack what you already have and per-store prices in Ingredients, so “in stock” badges and costs stay accurate.

Also handy: in My Fridge, tap the 📖 on any item for storage tips, and Nutrition shows weekly totals vs your goals. Sign in to sync across your phone and PC.
